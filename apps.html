APPLICATIONS:

Augmented Reality (AR) is used for a wide variety of purposes. Everything from the yard lines on the football field when you're watching a game on television (Augment.com) to Google's ability to translate live images (translate.google.com) are common uses of augmented reality in everyday life. Over the years, AR has become more prevalent in public use. The technology that was once only available in college labratories and government facilities is now available on the average smart phone. (Augment.com)

With widespread availability, augmented reality will only continue developing new uses and new technological advances. Major companies, like Apple and Google, have started incorporating augmented reality into their devices as well. (bgr.com) Their usage may, in the future, include identify objects and faces when the camera is pointed at them and showing the user a live gps view that overlays with what the camera is viewing to mark buildings and locations.

EXAMPLES

There are a large number of uses for AR technology. A few examples of these are:

  • Media
  • Video Games
  • Education
  • Tours and Site Seeing
  • Maps/GPS
  • Communication
  • Astronomy
  • Translation
