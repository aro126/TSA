AUGMENTED REALITY

DEFINITION:

Augmented Reality (AR) uses technology to change/enhance the way we view the world around us. According to livescience.com, "[AR superimposes] a computer-generated image [onto] a user's view of the real world, thus providing a composite view."

ORIGIN:

This technology has been around for awhile with the first recorded use in 1968 when Ivan Sutherland used it to show wireframe diagrams. (Augment.com) Since then, Augmented Reality has come a long way and is now used for far more than displaying diagrams.

On this website, the applications, history, and future uses of augmented reality will be discussed.
